Description

Warcraft's Banshee Queen Sylvanas Windrunner as she appears in Heroes of the Storm, by Blizzard Entertainment.

This pack includes:
- Sylvanas Windrunner
- High Elf Sylvanas
- Legion Sylvanas (AKA Warchief of the Horde)
- Ultimate Sylvanas
- Bloodraven Sylvanas (character from Diablo 2)
- Lunar Festival Sylvanas (also a Team Color variant)
- Sugar Plum Fairy Sylvanas

For a character who has a lot of variants most of them are very basic except for Sugar Plum Fairy, which also has to deal with a large tutu skirt that gets in the way of all of her crouching animations.

All of those instances of the Arrow skin are the same; you only need one of them.

"I thought I was the only one who'd been murdered by a cruel man, raised as a powerful but horrible abomination, subsequently crowned myself queen, and dedicated my subjects to orchestrating my vengeance. Then I met Kerrigan!"
